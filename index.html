1) What to Check

Team & Transparency

  • Doxed team (LinkedIn, GitHub, Google Scholar).
  • Prior crypto/tech success.
  • Consistent shipping through bear/bull.

Utility & Differentiation

  • Solves real blockchain pain points.
  • Clear niche: DeFi, RWAs, L2, staking, etc.
  • Roadmap + partnerships.

Tokenomics & Supply

  • Circulating vs total supply (ideal 1–5B).
  • Market cap = price × supply.
  • Low float pumps, risky dumps.

Vesting & Unlocks

  • Gradual unlocks > big cliffs.
  • Map team/VC unlock schedules.
  • Check market absorption.

Security & Governance

  • Audits (CertiK, Trail of Bits).
  • No unresolved exploits.
  • Staking risks, governance activity.

Community & Marketing

  • Organic activity, not paid hype.
  • Liquidity + healthy TVL.
  • Sentiment, whale flows.

5) Red Flags

  • Anonymous team, unverifiable advisors.
  • Short whitepaper, vague roadmap.
  • Low float + imminent unlocks.
  • Pure paid marketing/botted hype.
  • No audits, unresolved exploits.
  • Copy-paste utility, no edge.
  • Thin liquidity, high slippage.
  • Concentrated holder whales.

Tips & Notes
  • Market Cap = price × circulating supply
  • FDV = price × total supply
  • Rule of 3: need ≥3 strong signals (team, utility, tokenomics).
  • Accumulate in fear, distribute in greed.
